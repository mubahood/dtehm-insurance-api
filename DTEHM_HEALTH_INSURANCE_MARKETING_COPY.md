## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What happens if I need medical treatment?**
A: Your coverage activates immediately upon enrollment. Present your policy number at participating healthcare facilities for treatment of covered conditions.

**Q: How do I access the event equipment?**
A: Submit a booking request through the DTEHM app or contact customer service. Equipment is provided on a first-come, first-served basis.

**Q: When will I receive profit distributions?**
A: Profits from equipment rentals are distributed either bi-annually (every 6 months) or annually, depending on program performance.

**Q: What if I miss a monthly payment?**
A: You have a 7-day grace period. After that, coverage may be suspended until payment is received.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes, but early cancellation may forfeit certain benefits. Refer to terms and conditions for details.

**Q: Is the investment portion guaranteed?**
A: The investment portfolio generates returns based on actual rental business performance. While we maintain high-quality equipment to maximize profitability, returns are not guaranteed.
